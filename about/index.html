About Me

My name is 李昊轩 (Li Haoxuan), and I am currently pursuing a degree in Mechanical Design, Manufacturing, and Automation at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). I am passionate about robotics, embodied intelligence, and machine learning, with a strong focus on bringing theoretical research to practical applications.

Research Interests

  • Robot Learning & Embodied Intelligence: Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models, end-to-end robot manipulation
  • Deep Reinforcement Learning: Policy learning, sim-to-real transfer, sample-efficient RL
  • Computer Vision for Robotics: 3D perception, object detection, visual servoing
  • Embedded Systems & Robot Control: Real-time control, sensor fusion, edge deployment

Personal Statement

I am deeply passionate about robot learning and embodied intelligence. Throughout my undergraduate studies, I have progressed from engineering deployment of VLA models to gaining deeper insights into the core challenges of end-to-end robot learning: enabling robots to robustly perceive, decide, and execute in the real world.

My hands-on experience with competition projects and team collaboration has strengthened my ability to tackle complex technical problems and deliver practical solutions. I am eager to continue exploring cutting-edge research in robotics and contribute to advancing the field of embodied AI.
